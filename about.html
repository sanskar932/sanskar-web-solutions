My Story

I didn't learn to code to work in a city.

I grew up in Bahraich and I saw something really clear: local businesses here are doing great work but most of them have zero online presence. The baker who makes the best cake in town. The clinic where the doctor actually knows your name. The shop that's been running for 20 years. None of them have websites. None of them show up on Google.

That's what I'm fixing — one business at a time. I started Sanskar Web Solutions to be the web developer that Bahraich actually needed. Not some agency in Delhi that sends you a template and disappears. Someone you can call. Someone who shows up.

I've been building websites for 3+ years now. From simple one-page sites to multi-page business portals with WhatsApp ordering, booking systems, and AI chatbots. I've worked with bakeries, clinics, restaurants, coaching centres, and retail shops across Bahraich and nearby areas.

How I Work

Things I actually believe in

Honesty First

If your idea won't work or something is outside my scope, I'll tell you upfront. No false promises, no inflated quotes just to get the business.

Mobile Always Comes First

Most of your customers are on their phones. Every website I build is designed for mobile screens first — then scaled up for desktops.

Speed Matters

A slow website loses customers. I optimise every page so it loads fast even on slower mobile networks common in semi-urban areas like Bahraich.

I Teach You, Not Lock You In

After delivery, I walk you through how to use your website. You shouldn't need to call me every time you want to change a phone number.

Local Understanding

I know Bahraich. I know how people here search, what they trust, and what makes them pick up the phone or walk into your shop.

Your Time is Valuable

I don't drag projects on for weeks. I ask the right questions upfront, work fast, and keep you updated so there are no surprises at the end.
