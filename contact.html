Common Questions

Things people usually ask me first

How long does a website take?
A simple one-page site takes 48–72 hours. A full multi-page business website takes 5–7 days. Complex projects with backend systems take 2–3 weeks. I'll give you an exact timeline before we start.
Do I need to pay everything upfront?
No. I take 50% advance and 50% on delivery. Once you approve the final website and are happy with it, then you pay the balance. That's it.
What if I don't know what I want?
That's totally fine — most people don't. WhatsApp me, tell me what your business does, and I'll suggest what kind of website would actually help you. No pressure, no confusing tech talk.
Will my website work on mobile?
Yes, always. Every website I build is mobile-first. I design for phone screens first, then for desktop. Most of your customers in Bahraich will visit on their phones.
Do you provide hosting and domain?
Yes. I handle domain registration, hosting setup, and deployment. I'll explain your options (free vs paid hosting) and what makes sense for your budget and traffic.
What happens after the website goes live?
I provide 30 days of free support after launch. After that, I offer monthly maintenance plans. Small fixes, content updates, WhatsApp me — I won't leave you stranded.
